Given a million escort ads, how can we detect near duplicates? Such micro ad groups are often signals of human trafficking (TP). How can we summarize them to convince law enforcement to act? Detection of microgroups of near-duplicate documents is useful in multiple additional configurations, including detection of spam bots in Twitter ads, plagiarism, and more.
Blockchain technology is used to support digital assets such as cryptocurrencies and tokens. Commonly, smart contracts are used to generate tokens on the blockchain network. There are two fundamental types of tokens: fungible and non-fungible (NFT). This document focuses on NFT and offers a technique to detect plagiarism in NFT images. NFTs are information that is attached to files to produce distinctive signatures. It can be found in image archives, actual artifacts, published literature online, and various other digital media.
